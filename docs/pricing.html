Questions?

Can I change plans later?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What counts as a quote generation?

Each time you submit a lead (text or photo) and Tidy-Quote generates a quote draft, that counts as one generation.

What happens when I hit my monthly limit?

You can still access your pricing templates and previous quotes. To generate new quotes, upgrade your plan or wait for the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

The Starter plan at $1.99/month is designed to let you try Tidy-Quote with minimal commitment. No long-term contract.

Do you store my customer messages?

Messages are processed to generate quotes but are not stored long-term or used for AI training. Your data stays private.
